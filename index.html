Welcome to Athena

At Athena we know how difficult it can be to navigate the uncomfortable situations in the workplace environment.

You can unload your burden with us. From receiving questionable treatment to educating yourself on how to navigate situations diplomatically or correcting a misunderstanding, we provide guidance for all.

We strive to protect your privacy and anonymity when you want us to.

Frequently asked question

Does it store my private data?

We only store your data if you want us to. Your data will last for the duration of your session. There is also the option to volunteer your session to become aggregate data. With Athena, you're in control of your information.

Will my employer know what I share to you?

Your privacy is our highest priority and we will only allow your employer's Human Resources department access when you want us to. If you want us to only provide your employer with aggregate data in order to protect your identity, we will proceed to do so.

Is your information legally liable?

Athena will provide the recommended actions but also provide the potential consequences for those suggestions. Ultimately the action you take after a session with Athena is your own.

Can I track my incidents with you?

Absolutely. Documenting incidents is important to building your personal case and we will only keep your information if you want us to for your future reference.

Ready to start now?

Athena is absolutely free to the individual and allows you to ask questions, gain actionable advice, and report incidents in the workplace environment. Your privacy and safety is of the utmost importance to us. We will always prompt you about your personal information.
